Health, Safety, and Environment

It has been reported by the International Labour Organization (ILO) that more than 2.3 million workers die every year due to occupational accidents or work-related diseases. The ILO estimates that the yearly cost to the global economy from incidence alone is about $3 trillion. Hence, preventing illnesses and accidents caused by work should be a vital priority for employers and employees.

SOLMOJAK LIMITED uses her best practices to ensure QHSES performance measurement, improved organizations Safety culture, Safety management by recognizing the hazard and safety analysis, and sharing Safety data collection.

Our experts always ensure:
✓ workplace Health and Safety to reduce the avoidable illness and injury cost.
✓ improve your HSE safety culture, and enhance health and safety-related decision making for an efficient and effective operation.

Human resource is a very essential asset of every workplace that needs to be safeguarded by implementing a robust health & safety program.
