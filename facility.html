Facility Management Services

Facility Management has proven in recent and over time that the core business can be solely focused on productively while the occupants of the facility still enjoys the functionality, safety and comfort of the facility.

SOLMOJAK LIMITED is committed to bringing safety, comfort and functionality to your facility using her best practices of facility management services for organizational improvement and productivity.

Furthermore, SOLMOJAK LIMITED stands to address:
Distractions from core business areas due to frequent facility management issues.
High Power generation cost
Unexplained costs that result in a lack of transparency.
Difficulty in tracking your maintenance request leading to a long time on closing maintenance requests.

Our professionals always ensure:
Safe and clean working environment.
Functionality and comfort of your facility.

At Solmojak Limited

We offer the following quality facility management services to all Clients:

Cleaning and Waste Management

Our well trained janitor/cleaners provide quality cleaning services to health, safety and environmental standards. We offer waste collection and disposal through approved bodies for waste collection.

Security Services

We provide professional security guards that protects your facility and also ensuring good access control and emergency response both day and night. Enjoy 24/7 maximum security of your facility while we provide physical security guards, access to control systems & CCTV cameras for surveillance.

Power Management

We shall deliver to you and your organization, outstanding trainings, safety documentation, safety audit, safety management manual, man power outsourcing, and fire safety to upgrade your safety lifestyle.

Renovations

Our renovation services are designed to breathe new life into your spaces, creating a captivating and functional environment that exceeds your expectations. With a team of skilled professionals, we specialize in revitalizing homes, offices, and commercial properties to suit your unique vision and requirements.

Electrical installation

From precise wiring and fixture installations to a strong focus on safety and energy efficiency, our certified electricians deliver expert results that power your spaces with utmost reliability.

Plumbing Works

Experience top-notch plumbing services tailored to your needs. Our skilled team of professionals provides comprehensive solutions including installation, repair, and maintenance, ensuring efficient water supply and seamless waste management for your residential or commercial space.

Carpentry Works

Our expert team of carpenters delivers custom designs, precise installations, and meticulous craftsmanship for all your residential or commercial woodworking projects.
