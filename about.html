About Us

SOLMOJAK Limited is a facility management, Health Safety, and Environment Consultancy, and Branding company that specializes in offering cleaning, security, gardening, Maintenance and Repair services, and Health, Safety and Environment consultancy services to all facilities. It was established in March 2021.

Are you in need of proper maintenance and management of your facility for productivity, safety and comfort of your workers and occupants of your facilities? Then welcome to SOLMOJAK Limited. SOLMOJAK Limited provides quality facility management solutions and safety consultancy services to all kinds of facilities.

These services include:
— Effective cleaning services to offices, homes, and public places.
— Power Management, Maintenance of electrical systems, plumbing works, Renovation of Buildings among others
— Occupational health, Safety and Environment Consultancy, taking the safety of lives and properties as the highest priority.

The safety of people is much more important than anything else. The near miss reported today is the accident that doesn't occur tomorrow.
