Solomon Akpa

Director General/CEO

Solomon Akpa is the Director General and CEO at Solmojak Limited. He is a graduate of Federal University of Agriculture (now Joseph Sarwuan Tarka University), Makurdi. He specializes in Facility management, Quality Health Safety Environment and Sustainability, and has demonstrated history of working in the consulting industry. He is a British International Safety Organization trained Safety Professional.

He is a member of the Nigeria Society of Engineers(GMNSE), member of the International Association of Engineers(IAE). Additional to his diversity, he is a seasoned Brand Identity Designer, and Full-stack Web Developer.
